day 3 - illusion of me

okay , yeah , this might seem a little off considering i was just complaining about haircare - but hear me out .

i used to want to be invisible when i was a kid - i was shy and socially anxious , and living like i wanted to with no one's eyes on me

seemed so exciting .

i don't think that anymore .

i think that would get lonely for sure !

instead , my idea is to be able to live how i want , but with a fake version of myself appearing to others .

so if my hair is frizzy . . . who the fuck cares ?

i can just appear to everyone else as if i had spent hours retouching , brushing , moisturizing , etc .

of course this could be used for evil - but evil takes effort , doesn't it ? effort that i'm honestly too lazy to make .
